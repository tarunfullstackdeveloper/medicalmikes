Our Story

Started with a sick dog.
Built on honest medicine.

Medical Mike's began in 2019 when a Wall Street detour, an arthritic Saint Bernard, and a stubborn belief that CBD could be better all collided in Westchester County.

The Beginning

A Wall Street kid with a cannabis dream

Despite a trajectory toward finance, Mike never stopped thinking about hemp. In 2017 — when CBD was still federally illegal — he enrolled in a cannabis business course at a university in Maryland, driving back and forth from New York every week.

His professor thought he was crazy. He was right. But after the Farm Bill passed in 2018, Medical Mike's launched almost immediately. The mission was simple: premium CBD that people could actually afford, from a source they could actually trust.

The Catalyst

Torre changed everything

Mike's Saint Bernard, Torre, suffered from early-onset arthritis. The vet recommended anti-inflammatory drugs, but they left her with stomach pains and ulcer risks. It wasn't worth it.

So Mike tried CBD. The difference was immediate — Torre slept through the night again. She wasn't in pain. That personal experience became the fuel behind every product Medical Mike's has made since.

The Farm

We grow it ourselves

The early days were rough. Mike's first supplier manufactured a batch of CBD rollers that didn't dispense. He sat on the floor manually poking holes in each one.

Today, Medical Mike's works exclusively with small, sustainable organic hemp farms in New York. Farm-to-bottle means no complicated supply chain — we know exactly where every drop comes from, and we can stand behind it.

"I saw the CBD market becoming saturated with fake products at crazy prices. I wanted Medical Mike's to be a source for people to access premium quality CBD they could actually afford."

— Mike, Founder
